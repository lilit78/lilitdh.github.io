Hailing from Middle East, of an Armenian descent, I traveled thousands of miles to California to pursue my design career. I’m currently a freelance designer based in Orange County California. My areas of focus are in web design, user experience design, and brand development.

Over the past 8 years I’ve had the chance to work with brands such as Samsung, MasterCard, Regal Cinemas, Medtronic Plum Organics, Nestlé and Sony Entertainment.

My goal is to always create simple and engaging designs, aimed at the finest user experience.
